> A psychologist‑informed, gamified task tracker that replaces anxiety with XP, guilt with levels, and procrastination with small, rewarding quests.

---

## ✨ What makes LifeQuest different

- **No punishment for skipping** – You can postpone or reduce any quest. The game rewards showing up, not perfection.
- **Coaching questions built‑in** – Before starting a quest, you’re asked: *“What will I miss if I don’t do this?”*, *“How will it affect my loved ones?”*, *“What becomes possible?”* – questions that turn tasks into transformation.
- **Life spheres & separate leveling** – Level up your Health, Work, Creativity, Relationships… each sphere progresses independently.
- **Sacred minimum & Sunday ritual** – 3–7 tiny daily habits (win every day) + weekly big chores that only appear on Sundays.
- **Epic quests with subtasks** – Break big projects into small steps, assign custom colors, track progress.
- **Time blocking calendar** – Plan your day with colored blocks, save weekly templates, see your history.
- **Complete privacy** – All data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
- **Works offline** – Install as PWA and use anywhere, even without internet.

## 📱 Install on your phone (free, 30 seconds)

### iPhone (Safari)
1. Open this page in Safari  
2. Tap the **Share** icon (square with arrow up)  
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**  
4. Tap **Add** – done!

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open this page in Chrome  
2. Tap the **three dots** menu (⋮)  
3. Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**  
4. Follow the prompts

> After installation, launch LifeQuest from your home screen – it becomes a standalone app.
